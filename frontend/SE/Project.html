Stock Forecaster Description

Stock forecasting is the act of trying to determine the future value of a company stock or other financial instrument traded on a financial exchange.The successful prediction of a stock's future price could yield significant profit. Our project aims at easing the task of investing in the virtual market by predicting the future price value based on historic price. This is mainly devised to help new enthusiastic investors/individuals who plan to invest into the market, with or without prior experience.

Our project will use predictive algorithms to perform price prediction. These algorithms run as a back-end task and compute the prediction values for the various stocks.

The project mainly focuses on the following aspects:

  1. Real-Life data feed:

    2. We use real life data from google finance. Prediction is hence based on real data feeding and the history of data, thus making it a realistic prediction history. The current stock quotes are then displayed to the end user with the help of charts for better understanding.

  2. Web Services:

    3. A class with methods that will do the following: 1. Show current stock quotes 2. Print graphs 3. Predict stock for short-term investment 4. predict stock for long-term investment

  3. Stock prediction:

    4. Prediction algorithms for long-term and short-term predictions. It helps the investor in deciding whether to BUY, HOLD or SELL a stock. Prediction is typically based on technical analysis. It uses information of past stock behaviour in order to forecast future price movements.

  4. News Feed:

    5. If any infliction points are found in the graphs, their possible causes are found using live news feed from around the globe.
